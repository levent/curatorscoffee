Our Design

The Curators Coffee design concept originated from our attempts to visualise the art of 'curating' coffee by developing spaces that fuel creativity and innovation.

This vision has been brought to life by talented local furniture builder Jason Prain of madebyjason and his leading design partners.

A collaboration between Jason and New Zealand based designer Jen Archer led to Curators Coffee Studio. A space of free thought, where things are collected and reimagined. To reflect this we've used a mix of recycled, new and artisan materials.

Our second store, Curators Coffee Gallery, was designed by Ana Foster-Adams. The high ceiling and plentiful natural light is the perfect environment for us to showcase a range of coffee equipment that you can take home or try at our brew bar. We also display innovative works from local artists that are available to purchase.

London's Best Coffee app says Curators Coffee Gallery is "relaxing, refreshing, and a little fantastical; a secret garden within what is becoming an increasingly coffee saturated hub in Fitzrovia."

Caffeine magazine says "there is a genuine absence of café cliché…the interior is bold, energetic and refreshingly clean in its lines and colours."
