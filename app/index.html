Our Oxford Circus branch is open this holiday period from 28th to 30th from 10am to 6pm!

Curators Coffee is a specialty coffee house that was created by a small group of Antipodeans who are devoted to great tasting coffee. In March 2012, Curators Coffee Studio opened its doors in the City of London. Our second venture, Curators Coffee Gallery, opened near Oxford Circus in June 2014.

We serve specialty coffees, teas and freshly prepared food from our in-house kitchen and a range of leading local suppliers. The latest brewing equipment and retail coffee beans are also available to take home.
