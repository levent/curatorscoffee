Our Coffee

We take great pride in preparing our coffee and have the pleasure of working with specialist roasteries that purchase direct from the world's leading farms. Giving us a wide range of high quality and delicate profiles that are roasted to perfection.

We are proud to introduce "Exhibition", our signature house blend roasted by the talented team at Nude Espresso.

This coffee is so delicious it gives new meaning to the words caffeine addiction.

Our Tea

Our teas are supplied by tea traders Lalani & Co who spend time every year visiting tea farms around the world to source only the highest quality teas. We brew our teas using exact recipes to ensure we get the best out of the leaves.

Our Food

Our food menu is designed by Head Chef, Josephine Hicks, and offers a range of simple, freshly prepared options throughout the day that are made from only the highest quality ingredients.

Our pastries are supplied by Yeast Bakery who are uncompromising in their pursuit of creating the perfect croissant. Focusing solely on butter croissants, almond croissants and pains au chocolat, there isn't a single other bakery that could match the care, attention (and taste!) that Yeast put into their products.

Fresh bread is delivered daily from Sally Clarke's Bakery. Served at breakfast as toast, and at lunch as a part of our freshly made toasted sandwiches.

Please check our Weekend Brunch Menu.
