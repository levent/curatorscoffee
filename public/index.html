In a burst of colour Curators Coffee Studio recently opened it's doors in the City of London. Devoted to specialty coffee, this new cafe has been created by a collection of people who all love great tasting coffee. Unique design is central to the Curators Coffee ethos and their innovative, fresh approach provides a stark contrast to the usual environment of other city cafes. Combined with their great service, it's an uplifting coffee experience.

Our Coffee

We take great pride in preparing our coffee and have the pleasure of working with local London roasteries who support direct trade methods of purchasing coffee, resulting in a range of high quality of coffees on offer, all roasted to perfection using delicate roasting profiles.

Our house espresso is the Rogue from Union Hand-Roasted Coffees. Developed with Andrew Tolley of Taylor St Baristas, the components of Rogue change on a seasonal basis which ensure a great tasting product at all times of the year.

Our guest espresso, decaf and filter coffee is provided by Square Mile Coffee Roasters. What we offer in store will constantly change to showcase their stunning range of coffees.

For more details ask our baristas what is currently being featured.

Our Tea

Our teas are supplied by tea traders Lalani & Co who spend time every year visiting tea farms around the world to source only the highest quality teas. We brew our teas in homemade Gaiwans using exact recipes to ensure we get the best out of the leaves.

We also make a fresh mint infusion made from our mint garden grown in store. We have a range of mint including Applemint and Black Peppermint so ask our staff what they can offer you.

Our Food

Our food menu is designed around the concept of having simple options made from good quality ingredients.

Our Pastries are supplied by Yeast Bakery who are uncompromising in their pursuit of creating the perfect croissant.

The remainder of our food which includes delicious sweet and savoury muffins, decadent brownies and custard tarts, classic tasty sandwiches made on a range of breads and fresh seasonal salads, come from Browns of Brockley.

For more information on our current food offering and enquiries for office catering please email food@curatorscoffee.com.
