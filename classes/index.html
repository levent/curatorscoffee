Brewed Coffee Class

Working from home and weekends shouldn’t mean you have to miss out on your morning coffee. Filtered coffee is really simple to make and tastes great if you follow a few simple guidelines!

We will be running Work From Home Coffee Classes to teach you the basics of making tasty filtered coffee.

Classes go for 1 hour and you will learn how to make filtered coffee using French Press, Aeropress and Pourover methods.

Class costs £15 per person and there is a maximum of 8 people per class.

All of our classes are held at Curators Coffee Gallery.

Latte Art Class

Attempt to pour your own amazing latte art images and then marvel at how terrible you are. Don't worry, we will be there to guide you and provide words of encouragement.

The class will cover basic milk chemistry and milk texturing but will focus mainly on Latte art pouring.

Latte Art Classes cost £30 per person and go for 1.5 hours and are limited to a maximum of 4 people per class to ensure everyone gets plenty of practice.

All of our classes are held at Curators Coffee Gallery.

Tea Masterclass

The Masterclass hosted by Lalani & Co is an introduction to the world of single batch tea. Each tea is sourced from prime seasons from small family owned gardens and exhibit unique expressions of the terroir.

The class will include a tasting of of five unique single batch teas and your own jar of Premium Assam from the Belseri Estate to take home (valued at £10). The class is £25 per person for 1.5 hours tasting of some of the most exquisite teas in the world from Darjeeling to Kyoto.

All of our classes are held at Curators Coffee Gallery.

Matcha Masterclass

Lalani & Co present the ways to make Matcha. Drink traditional styles while mastering the technique. Taste Curators famous Matcha Latte and find out what it works best with in smoothies and juices. Learn how to use it and then you get to go home with a jar of Lalani & Co Matcha Gold the ultimate shaded and stone milled Matcha green tea. (included in the ticket RRP £29)

All of our classes are held at Curators Coffee Gallery.
