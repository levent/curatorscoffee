Curators Coffee could be in your next meeting!

There is nothing more exciting than a meeting, except maybe a meeting with your own in-office specialty coffee experience!

We can provide hot single origin filter coffee, fresh orange juice, and a range of cakes that would be perfect for a mid morning or afternoon meeting. Please see our full range below and give us a call on 020 7283 4642 or email contact@curatorscoffee.com for a quote or to place an order.

For orders made more than 48hrs in advance we are able to provide pastries suitable for Breakfast meetings as well.

And if you have any specific requests, like whole cakes (for birthdays) please give us a call and we will do our best to help you out.
